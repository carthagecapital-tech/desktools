Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in design and typesetting since the 1500s. It's derived from a passage by Cicero written in 45 BC, deliberately scrambled so readers focus on visual layout rather than reading the content. The standard passage begins with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit."

Designers use placeholder text to fill layouts during prototyping when final copy isn't ready. Real text can distract from design decisions about typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy. Lorem Ipsum has a roughly natural distribution of letter frequencies and word lengths, making it look more realistic than repeating "text here" or "asdf."

Frequently asked questions

How do I generate lorem ipsum placeholder text?

Pick the unit (Paragraphs, Sentences, or Words), set the count, and click Generate. Copy the output with one click. Works for mockups, wireframes, and UI prototypes where you need filler text.

What is lorem ipsum and why is it used?

Lorem ipsum is scrambled Latin text that's been used as placeholder copy since the 1500s. Designers use it because it looks like real text (proper word lengths, sentence structure) but is meaningless, so reviewers focus on the design rather than reading the content.

How much lorem ipsum should I use for a design mockup?

Enough to approximate the real content's length. A blog post mockup: 3-5 paragraphs. A product card: 2-3 sentences. A paragraph of body text: 50-100 words. Getting the length right helps the mockup reveal spacing and overflow issues you'd miss with a single short sentence.

Is lorem ipsum Latin? What does it actually mean?

It's derived from a 1st-century BC Cicero text (De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum), but scrambled and modified so it's not translatable. The common first line "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet" comes from a fragment of the original, cut mid-word. It means nothing as a coherent passage.

Should I ship lorem ipsum to production?

Never. Real designs need real content — placeholder text hides many layout problems (too-long actual copy, localization issues, overflow). Also: clients occasionally approve mockups thinking the lorem is the real content. Always replace before launch.
