Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in design and typesetting since the 1500s. It's derived from a passage by Cicero written in 45 BC, deliberately scrambled so readers focus on visual layout rather than reading the content. The standard passage begins with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit."

Designers use placeholder text to fill layouts during prototyping when final copy isn't ready. Real text can distract from design decisions about typography, spacing, and visual hierarchy. Lorem Ipsum has a roughly natural distribution of letter frequencies and word lengths, making it look more realistic than repeating "text here" or "asdf."

This tool generates Lorem Ipsum by paragraphs, sentences, or word count. For web design mockups, 2-3 paragraphs per section is typical. For testing text overflow, long paragraphs stress-test your CSS. Some developers prefer using real content from day one — but for quick wireframes, Lorem Ipsum remains the industry standard.
